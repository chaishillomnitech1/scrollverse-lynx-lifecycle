# DAO Governance Onboarding Template

## Introduction
Welcome to the DAO Governance community! This document outlines the onboarding process for new members getting involved in DAO governance. 

## Goals
- Understand the roles and responsibilities within the DAO.
- Familiarize yourself with the governance structure.
- Learn how to participate in governance decisions.

## Steps
1. **Read the DAO Charter**  
   Familiarize yourself with the vision and mission of the DAO.

2. **Join the Community**  
   Participate in discussions on our community forums and communication channels.

3. **Understand the Governance Process**  
   Review how proposals are made and voted on.

4. **Engage in Governance**  
   Start participating in discussions and contribute to proposals.
